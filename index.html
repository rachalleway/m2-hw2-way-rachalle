Hello! My name is Rachalle!

A Bit About Me

I was born in Henderson, Nevada about 20 minutes from the glittery streets of Las Vegas. When I was in first grade, I moved to Melbourne, Florida. I recently graduated the University of Florida with a Bachelor's in Advertsing. I'm currently working part time as an assistant graphic designer at a small, remote digital marketing agency. I'm also working full time as a graduate student hoping to earn an MA in Mass Communications with a Concentration in Web Design by the end of Fall 2023.

For the Love of Design

Since I was young, I remember always having a love for creativity and art. This love only blossomed during my time at UF where I explored my great and growing passion for graphic design. During my time at UF, I designed for many student organizations and publications as a graphic designer where I was able to develop my skills and explore the intricacies of what it's like to design for a team. One of the publications I designed for was Rowdy Magazine, the nation's largest student-run magazine for pop culture, fashion, and politics.

What I Love Most About Design

  1. There's always room for improvement
  2. You don't have to stick to one style of design
  3. The type of project you're working on always changes
  4. Design is a solution to a problem and always serves a purpose
  5. Design is strategic

Where I Hope to Go

After I finish my MA, I hope to work full time as a graphic designer, web designer, or product designer.
